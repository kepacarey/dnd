Saylor – Divine Feline Companion

Tiny Beast (celestial, disguised), Neutral Good

Armor Class 13 (natural armor)

Hit Points 9 (2d6 + 2)

Speed 40 ft., climb 30 ft.

STR 3 (–4) DEX 16 (+3) CON 12 (+1)

INT 10 (+0) WIS 16 (+3) CHA 14 (+2)

Skills Perception +5, Stealth +5, Insight +5, Arcana +2

Damage Resistances necrotic, radiant

Condition Immunities disease

Senses passive Perception 15

Languages Understands Common and Celestial (cannot speak)

Purr of Peace (1/long rest). One ally gains +1d4 to a hit die during a short rest.

Distracting Meow (Recharge 5–6). One creature within 30 ft. must make a DC 13 Wisdom save or suffer disadvantage on its next attack roll.

Instinctual Guardian (1/day). When an ally within 10 ft. would drop to 0 HP, they drop to 1 HP instead.

Unnatural Awareness. Saylor subtly reacts to illusions, cursed magic, or divine presence.

Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1 slashing damage.
